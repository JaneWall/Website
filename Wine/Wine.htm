Some of my Favorite Wines

 

FRANCE

Burgundy

Christoph Bellang in Meursault: Volnay 1er Cru, Savigny les Beaune

Albert Boillot (Volnay) - ruelle Saint Etienne, 21190 Volnay, Louis Boillot, Tel. 03.80.21.61.21: Volnay Chapelle 1er Cru

Gabriel Billard in Pommard : Bourgogne Pinot Noir, Pommard Vaumuriens, Beaune Epenottes 1er Cr.  Excellent wines, lovely winemaker.  They need a little age before drinking.  Predominant cherry notes.

Camus Pere & Fils in Gevrey-Chambertin: Mazy-Chambertin Grand Cru - top level red wines, earthy.

Chene-Colombe: Macon-Villages - nice white wine for everyday drinking.  Laura's favorite!  Goes great with turkey.

Dugat-Py in Gevrey-Chambertin - one of the most exclusive, fabulous, impossible to find wines ever.

  This is the bottle David and Randi gave me for Christmas, 2010.  Drank it with David in Spet., 2011.  Was amazing!!

Charles Gardet in Champagne: Brut, Rose Saignee 2002

Claudie Jobard: Rully Clos St. Jacques 1er Cru - very nice affordable 1er cru.

Jean-Luc Joillot in Pommard: Cremant de Bourgogne ("Champagne method") - my favorite affordable sparkling wine.

Paul Jouard in Chassagne-Montrachet - fantastic white wines, particularly Les Chaumees.  Buy anything he makes!

Clos des Lambrays - Morey St. Denis - fantastic red, earthy wine.  hard to find and pricey.

Francois Leclerc in Gevrey-Chambertin: Bourgogne, Les Corbeaux 1er Cru - Corbeaux has a grand cru taste at 1er cru prices ... hard to beat this wine.  One of my all-time favorites!

Rene Leclerc in Gevrey-Chambertin: Bourgogne, Gevrey-Chambertin

Domaine des Malandes in Chablis - 63 rue Auxerroise, 89800 Chablis, 03.86.42.41.37 Lynne Marchive - favorite chablis producer ... she makes really fantastic white wines.  try the Vaudesir!

Arnaud Margaine  in Champagne - 3 Avenue de Champagne, 51380 Villers Marmery; 03.26.97.92.13 - love his blanc de blanc champagne!  great small producer.

Anne Parent in Pommard: Bourgogne Pinot Noir, Ladoix 1er Cru La Corvee, Volnay 1er Cru Clos des Chenes, Les Chaponneires 1er Cru - love all her wines!  particularly the volnay!!

Alain Patriarche in Meursault: Bourgogne Chardonnay La Monatine, Meursault Vieilles Vignes

Francois Servin in Chablis: Special Reserve Chablis, Vaillons 1er Cru, Grand Cru Les Clos

Chantal Tortochot in Gevrey-Chambertin: Lavaux St Jacques 1er Cru, Chambertin Grand Cru - Chantal makes some of the best wines around.  Full-bodied, earthy pinots.
